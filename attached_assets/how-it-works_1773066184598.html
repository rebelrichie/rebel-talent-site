FAQ

Half the length, twice the punch.

"Fractional Head of Talent" = ?
Strategic leadership without the full-time salary. I embed with your team (weekly syncs, ATS access), build your recruiting machine, fill your hardest roles, then scale down or exit.
Agency vs. Me?

Agency: Transactional. Paid per hire. Gone after placement.

Me: Embedded. Infrastructure + execution. Measured by retention and pipeline velocity.

If you need 1-2 hires per year, Underground Admins is a better fit. If you're scaling and need talent infrastructure, Rebel Talent is what you need.

Contingency?

No. I build systems. Contingency is for order-takers.

If you need speed and one-off contingent placements, Underground Admins can handle that separately.

How long?
3-6 months to build + fill. Month-to-month after. Walk anytime.
Company size?

Series A-C startups, defense contractors. 20-200 people scaling fast but not ready for full-time TA leadership.

If you're hiring 1-2 people per year, Underground Admins is a better fit. If you're hiring 10+ and need talent strategy, we should talk.

Roles?
Technical, GTM, ops, leadership. Cleared/DoD specialist. No high-volume hourly.
Already have a recruiter?
Perfect. I build what they execute within. Or handle overflow when they're slammed.
After I'm gone?
You own everything. Systems, docs, pipelines, trained team. No vendor lock-in.
